Overview

High-performance polyurethane flooring solutions designed to provide superior durability, chemical resistance, and a seamless finish for demanding industrial environments.

Cold & Dry Storage PU Flooring

Specially engineered for temperature-controlled environments. This system withstands thermal shock and provides a slip-resistant surface perfect for food storage and warehouses.

Dressing Plant PU Flooring

Hygiene-focused flooring that meets strict safety standards. Resistant to heavy organic acids, fats, and frequent high-pressure cleaning, ideal for food processing facilities.

Hatchery PU Flooring

Durable and easy-to-sanitize surfaces designed for agricultural settings. Provides long-term protection against moisture and chemicals while maintaining a clean environment.
