Overview

High-performance polyurethane flooring designed for durability, hygiene, and resistance to heavy usage. Ideal for environments that require seamless, non-slip, and easy-to-clean surfaces.

Cold & Dry Storage PU Flooring

Designed to withstand low temperatures and thermal shock, this system prevents cracking and maintains strong adhesion even in extreme cold storage conditions. Perfect for food storage facilities.

Dressing Plant (Chicken) PU Flooring

Hygienic and anti-slip flooring solution built for wet and high-traffic areas. Resistant to chemicals, blood, and frequent washdowns—ideal for poultry processing environments.

Hatchery PU Flooring

Seamless and easy-to-sanitize flooring that supports strict cleanliness standards. Provides a safe, bacteria-resistant surface suitable for sensitive hatchery operations.
